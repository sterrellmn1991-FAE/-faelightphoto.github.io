The Photographer

Raw light.
Real ground.

I'm Steve Terrell — a writer, musician, and photographer based in Minneapolis. I shoot low and slow, from the ground up, because that's where the truth lives. Fae Light is how I share that truth: nature and wildlife caught in the moments before you'd look away.

Every print is a reminder that the extraordinary was always right there — you just had to be willing to kneel.
