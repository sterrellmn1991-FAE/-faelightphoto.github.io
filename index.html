A Personal Note

I started carrying a camera because I believed the world had more beauty in it than most people were stopping to see. Not the kind of beauty that announces itself — the quiet kind. The kind that lives at ground level, in the spaces between things, in the light that only lasts a few seconds before it's gone forever.

If you've made it this far — if something here stopped you, even for a moment — then this work did exactly what it was made to do. Thank you for seeing it. Thank you for being here. That means more than I know how to say.

With gratitude, Steve Terrell  ·  Fae Light Photography
